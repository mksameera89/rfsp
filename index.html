Introduction

Introduction to the Rubber Farming Service Provider Program

The Rubber Farming Service Provider program, spearheaded by the Advisory Services Department of the Rubber Research Institute Sri Lanka, is a transformative initiative aimed at empowering individuals through comprehensive training in the dynamic field of rubber farming.

Program Overview:

This groundbreaking initiative begins by carefully selecting 50 unemployed individuals, providing them with a unique opportunity to undergo extensive training. Covering crucial aspects of rubber farming, the program delves into topics such as land preparation, establishment of fences, excavation pits, planting procedures, application of fertilizers, and much more.

Training Highlights:

Participants engage in a holistic learning experience, combining theoretical understanding with practical application. The curriculum is meticulously designed to equip individuals with expertise in vital areas, including identifying nutritional deficiencies, recognizing diseases and pests, and implementing effective treatments. Moreover, hands-on experience is gained in essential tasks such as tree marking, fixing rain guards, and the process of making compost.

Issuance of Certificates:

Upon successful completion of the program, participants receive certificates endorsed by the Rubber Research Institute. These certificates serve not only as a testament to the acquired skills but also as a key to unlocking new opportunities within the rubber farming sector.

Bridging Gaps in the Rubber Community:

Armed with their certificates, the trained individuals become ambassadors of expertise, connecting with rubber farmers, estates, and private companies involved in rubber farming. This network enables the seamless provision of specialized services, creating a synergy between knowledge and practical application.

Entrepreneurial Opportunities:

The program isn't just about training; it's about cultivating a new generation of service providers. These certified individuals have the potential to establish themselves as entrepreneurs within the rubber farming sector. By offering their specialized services, they not only contribute to the growth and efficiency of rubber farming practices but also pave the way for financial independence.

Join Us on the Path to Prosperity:

We extend an invitation to those with a passion for agriculture and a vision for success to embark on this transformative journey. Through the Rubber Farming Service Provider program, participants gain not only invaluable skills but also a passport to a world of opportunities within the rubber farming community. Together, let's cultivate prosperity, empower individuals, and create a thriving ecosystem within the rubber industry
